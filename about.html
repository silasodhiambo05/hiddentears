HTK SDG PURPOSE STATEMENT

We commit to uphold the value and prominence of humanity by fostering a culture of innovation among individuals (SDG9), promoting their well-being (SDG3) through counselling services and championing for gender equality (SDG 5) among communities. We will do so by providing quality education (SDG4) through mentorship, experiential training, capacity building activities as well as engaging global and local partners (SDG17) for sustainable development.
